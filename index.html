Week 10: Machine Learning - Unsupervised
The Human development index is based on life expectancy, education and GNI per capita. There are 4 tiers, low, medium, high, and very high. By performing K-means clustering with an Education index and Log of Tax per capita I would expect to be able to identify these tiers.
While the data follows a trend, there are not obviously distinct clusters. Nevertheless, out of curiosity I compared the cluster predictions to the real tiers and found that it was 72% accurate.
